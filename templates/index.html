HOW IT WORKS

Behavioral Intelligence for Pharmaceuticals

Four layers that transform clinical data into real-world usability intelligence.

01 — LIFESTYLE ENGINE

Life Graph Modeling

Captures work schedules, sleep patterns, diet, travel frequency, fasting habits, exercise, and social stress into a behavioral life graph.

02 — SIMULATION

Medication Twin

Runs the medication through the life graph — predicting dose conflicts, timing instability, interaction risks, and likely abandonment points.

03 — PHARMA DASHBOARD

Demographic Friction Maps

Aggregates simulations across demographic profiles to reveal high-risk subgroups — night workers, fasting populations, frequent travelers.

04 — PACKAGING INTEL

Delivery Optimization

Recommends blister redesigns, dosing timing changes, smart label innovations, and patient education updates based on detected friction patterns.

PharmaTwin is a simulation and intelligence platform. All outputs are predictive models for research purposes and do not constitute medical advice. · Built for pharmaceutical behavioral intelligence.
